Aglio Olio Recipe

Delicious Aglio Olio

Simple, elegant and delicious pasta. They key is to toast your garlic to a perfect golden brown.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 6 cloves garlic sliced thinly
  • Crushed red pepper flakes
  • Black Pepper
  • Fresh Parsely

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti in boiling water until al dente
  2. Toast garlic in olive oil, add spaghetti water to cook garlic for longer time without burning it.
  3. Drain pasta, add seasonings, then stir into garlic and oil.
  4. Serve
