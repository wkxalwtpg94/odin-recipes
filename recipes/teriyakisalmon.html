Teriyaki Salmon Recipe

Delicious Aglio Olio

Delicious healthy seared salmon. Kids love it! Pair it with some fried rice to complete your meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbps Dashi
  • 1 tbps Mirin
  • 1 tbp Cooking Sake
  • 1 tbps brown sugar
  • 1 tbps soy sauce
  • 2 Salmon steaks

Steps

  1. Mix dashi, mirin, sake, soy sauce and brown sugar.
  2. Marinate salmon in mixture. Save half of sauce.
  3. Sear salmon in pan until browned on both sides. Pour remaining sauce after turning off the heat as pan is cooling down.
  4. Serve hot on rice.
